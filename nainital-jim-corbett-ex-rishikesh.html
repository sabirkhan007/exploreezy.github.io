Overview

Explore the picturesque landscapes of Nainital and Jim Corbett on this enchanting 4-day journey. Starting with a scenic drive from Haridwar, this tour offers a delightful mix of natural beauty and adventure. Visit the serene lakes of Nainital, the charming hill station of Mukteshwar, and the iconic Jim Corbett National Park. Enjoy boat rides, temple visits, and the thrill of a wildlife safari. With comfortable accommodations, guided tours, and a focus on the tranquil beauty of Uttarakhand, this tour is perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

03 Nights/04 Days

Itinerary

Day 01: HARIDWAR – NAINITAL ( 250/5-6 hours)


    • On Arrival Haridwar pick up from Rishikesh then smooth drive to Nainital, On arrival check in at the hotel after fresh up visit Mall Road and enjoy boat ride in Naini Lake. Dinner and Overnight stay at the hotel.
    • Nainital: Nainital is a beautiful place that promises to calm your senses. It is a famous hill station in Uttarakhand, famous for it’s breath-taking beauty, lush green grasslands, mesmerising landscapes, emerald lakes, and ancient temples. The place is very popular for newly married couples who visit here fir Hani moon.

      Drive to Nainital

      Visit Mall Road and Naini Lake

      Stay at Nainital

Day 02: NAINITAL – BHIMTAL – SATTAL – NAINITAL860 kms/1-2 hours)


    • Morning after breakfast, spend whole day at Nainital, You can start your journey by visiting Cheena Peak where you can enjoy the breath-taking views of Himalayas. Then visit Hanumangarhi Temple which is dedicated to Lord Hanuman, the sacred shrines offer astonishing sunrise and sunset views, Later you can calm your mind by visiting beautiful lakes of Nainital which is Bhimtal, Sattal, Naukuchiatal which is very famous in Nainital for its scenic beauty and natural atmosphere, in the evening drive to Jim Corbett National Park, on arrival check in at the hotel. Dinner and overnight stay at hotel

    • Bhimtal: Bhimtal is famous for it’s beautiful lakeside destination that offers a peaceful environment to all tourists. It is a famous Hill station in Nainital because of its natural beauty and soothing atmosphere.

    • Sattal: Sattal is a place where seven lakes meets. The place is surrounded by beautiful green scenery and is famous for it’s breath-taking beauty and lush green grasslands.

      Visit to Bhimtal, Sattal and Naukuchiatal

      Visit Hanumangarhi Temple

      Visit Chandrashila

      Stay at Jim Corbett

Day 3: NAINITAL – MUKTESHWAR – JIM CORBETT(110 km/3-4 hours)


    • Morning after breakfast, visit a beautiful ancient Mukteshwar Temple which is situated in the Kumaon Hills, Mukteshwar is a popular tourist destination in Kumaon region. On arrival perform Puja and Darshan and after taking the blessings, you can visit the 75 years old Little Church, Also, you can enjoy a cable car ride to Snow View Point. In the evening drive towards Jim Corbett, on arrival check in at the hotel. Dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

    • Mukteshwar: Mukteshwar is famous for it’s lush green forests, Fruit orchards, lush green trails and narrow lanes and especially famous for it’s ancient Mukteshwar Temple which make it special destination for tourists who love nature and adventure apart from this Mukteshwar is also famous for trekking, camping, paragliding, rock climbing and rappelling. Mukteshwar has lots of holiday resorts from where you can see lots of peaks, you can also see Nanda Devi peak from there which is the second highest peak in India.

      Visit to Mukteshwar Temple

      Enjoy Cable car ride

      Stay at Jim Corbett

Day 04: JIM CORBETT – HARIDWAR (150 kms/3-4hours)


    • Early morning you can enjoy the Safari at your own cost and also can Explore the wild forest where you can see the beautiful sunrise and other eye-catching views of flora and fauna. Then after having breakfast at the hotel, check out from the Hotel and then our driver will drop you at Haridwar.

    • Jim Corbett: Jim Corbett National Park was established in 1936. It is located in the Himalayas’ foothills, near the Nainital, it is famous for the home to a large number of Bengal tigers, which is highest from any Indian national park. Jim Corbett is the home for more than 500 species of animals, reptiles, and birds. Including Tiger, Leopard, sloth Bear, Jungle Cat, Fishing Cat, Wild Boar, Langur, Rhesus Monkeys, Gharial, Mugger Crocodiles and many more. The place offers many things to those who is interested in nature closely. Tourists can enjoy Jeep Safari and Elephant Safari in the national park and their can spot various different species.

      Enjoy Morning Safari

      Drop at Rishikesh
