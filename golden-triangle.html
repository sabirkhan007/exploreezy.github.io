Overview

The India Golden Triangle Tour is a renowned travel circuit in India, encompassing the cities of Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur. Delhi, makes up one of the cornerstones of the golden triangle in the country's north. Agra is a medieval city on the banks of Yamuna River was founded by Sultan Sikandar Lodi in 1956.
